Brief Summary of the Elements on our site

Responsiveness

To address the responsiveness of the web pages, the following tips have been taken in to consideration:

  • Use the following Meta Tag: it gives the browser the instructions on how to control the page's dimension and scaling by setting the viewport of the page.
    meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1"
  • Using the width:100% instead of manually manipulating the dimension of some of the images. It allows the images to shrink to scale.
  • Font size of some of the page has been adjusted using "VW" as the unit of the size (viewport) so that the text sizze follows the size of the browser windows

Usability

To make our website easy to use for visitors, we added explanations and navigation buttons. These features allows visitors better understand our content and switch between different pages quickly. We also added pictures to let visitors see what breeds we are talking about, sicne not everyone is familiar with names. Additionlly, the pictures will allow unexperienced customers to get name-appearance associations.

Content

The overall goal of our website was to create a fluid and easy to use website emphasizing on our love for dogs. To ensure credibility and authenticity, the team members uploaded some of their dogs images to be used for the site. We also linked some credible external site where our visitors interested in adopting a dog can go to.

Design

While planning out our website, we emphasised on ensuring our visitors will have a unique experience. We designed this page in the manner that most of our visitors will be Animal lovers and or people who are starting to look into adopting dogs as their pets. As a result we layout our site using the following guidelines:

  • Simplicity: While the look and feel of our website is important to us considering the visiors, we simplified the the webpage by eliminating unnecssary elements that we know wouldn't appeal to the users. we added the following aspects to make the site more simple and easy to interact:
    • Colors
    • Graphics
    • Videos
  • Convinience: We made the navigation bar easier for the user to access at all times by making it fixed on the page as the scroll. In addition, the links in the navigation bar opens in a new tab, so that the users can be able to switch back and forth the content they are looking on the external link and our page.
  • Consistency: We ensured consistency on our site by maintaining the same background color and back color for the "Return to Homepage" button. This makes it easier for the user to return to the main page with ease.
